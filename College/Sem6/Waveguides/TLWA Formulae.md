# Antenna
## Radiation Pattern
### Radiation Density
$$\vec{w} = \vec{E} \times \vec{H}\ \frac{Watt}{m^2}$$
### Average Radiation Density

$$\vec{w}_{avg} = \frac{1}{2}[Re\{\vec{E} \times \vec{H} \}]$$
### Instantaneous Power
$$P_{inst} = \oint{\vec{w}.\vec{ds}}$$
### Average Power Density
$$P_{avg} = \oint \vec{w}_{avg}\ \cdot\ d\vec{s}$$
